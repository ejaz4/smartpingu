Smart Pingu

Your Smart Pingu has just been activated, please now configure your Pingu accordingly.

Step 1

Configure settings related to your network and internet connection.


Enter a number below to select the maximum number of devices you want connected to your home Wi-Fi network. If the number of devices falls above the number you set, you will be alerted.




This setting is experimental, some devices use a technology that keep them always connected to the network even when they're turned off.



You can configure Automations later to wake up devices when certain events are pulled.

Step 2

Configure settings related to your environment.


Set the maximum temperature you want the environment to get, before you are alerted to turn down your heating.





Set the maximum humidity % you want the environment to get, before you are alerted.
